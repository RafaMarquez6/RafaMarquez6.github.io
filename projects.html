We got this Go Kart and built it within the Belmont Wheels club. With my engineering teacher, Mr. Dyer, as the club advisor.

This is a kit, we bought it from ‘Drift Hero’, an online store for mini vehicles such as minibikes and go karts.

We raised $2,564 for this Kart by going door to door throughout Belmont and asking for donations. In addition, Mr. Dyer sent emails to organizations and school parents to raise money.

This was a great experience as I learned more about cooperation, suspension, and electric go kart components, which the previous go kart lacked.

Project Timeline: September–December, 2024
